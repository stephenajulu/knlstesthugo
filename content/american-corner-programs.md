### Community Gateways to Exchange & Innovation

**American Spaces** (American Corners) are collaborative partnerships between the **US Embassy in Kenya** and **KNLS**, providing access to digital learning, STEM tools, and educational opportunities.

Located inside select KNLS branches, these corners provide free access to computing labs, 3D printing equipment, VR headsets, and EducationUSA advising resources. Weekly activities feature expert panels, coding bootcamps, and career workshops, creating learning environments for youth and professionals.
