About the event

Showcasing Legal Deposit collections

The event will present a curated selection of legal deposit publications received by KNLS. Visitors will be able to discover recent Kenyan titles, understand how legal deposit supports national memory and learn why authors and publishers are encouraged to submit their works.

What visitors can expect

Event highlights

Visitor-friendly sections that explain the value of reading and legal deposit.

📚

Book showcase

Explore recent legal deposit titles from different authors, publishers and subject areas.

🏛️

Preserving heritage

Learn how KNLS preserves Kenya’s published works for future generations.

✍️

Author and publisher awareness

Understand the role of authors and publishers in submitting legal deposit copies.

👨‍👩‍👧

Public engagement

Encourage families, students and readers to discover new books and reading materials.

Programme

Event flow

The programme guides visitors through the showcase, reading activities and legal deposit awareness.

  1. Opening and welcomeIntroduce the reading day and Legal Deposit showcase.
  2. Legal Deposit showcase walk-throughGuide visitors through selected publications and explain their importance.
  3. Reading and discovery sessionInvite visitors to explore titles and learn about reading culture.
  4. Author, publisher and public engagementShare guidance on legal deposit submission and preservation.
