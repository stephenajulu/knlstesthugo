Overview

Capacity Building

KNLS capacity building initiatives equip individuals, institutions and communities with the tools they need to thrive in today’s knowledge economy through staff development, community training, partner-led projects and school support.

✦

Staff development

Workshops, leadership programmes, digital certifications and peer learning for service excellence.

✦

Community training

Digital literacy, job readiness, entrepreneurship, financial skills and soft skills programmes.

✦

Institutional support

Teacher support, mobile libraries, reading culture promotion and curriculum-aligned resources.
