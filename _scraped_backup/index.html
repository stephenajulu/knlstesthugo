About KNLS

Kenya National Library Service

KNLS is a Government State Corporation established under Cap 225 of the Laws of Kenya in 1965 and revised under Act No. 12 of 1984. Headquartered at Maktaba Kuu in Upper Hill, Nairobi, KNLS provides national library services, digital access and public information resources.
