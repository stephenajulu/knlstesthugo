### Expanding Resource Reach

KNLS coordinates **Interlibrary Loans** to help researchers and readers access materials that are not in our immediate collections.

By partnering with academic libraries, research institutes, and international information networks, we facilitate resource sharing. If you require specific books, archives, or research reports, our team can request transfers from partner institutions, making them available at your nearest KNLS branch.
