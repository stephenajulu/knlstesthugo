Our partner story

Behind every vibrant shelf is a partnership that made access possible.

Behind every vibrant library shelf, every child’s curious question, and every young person discovering new digital opportunities, there is a story of partnership. At KNLS, that story is grounded in collaboration and the belief that knowledge can change lives.

We are grateful to the organisations that have walked with us—bringing books, technology, inclusive tools, cultural exchange, infrastructure and youth-focused programmes to communities across Kenya.

📚

Reading access

Book donations, reading materials and library collections that widen access to learning.

💻

Digital inclusion

ICT infrastructure, digital resources and learning tools for connected communities.

♿

Inclusive libraries

Braille, audio resources, adaptive technologies and accessible spaces for all readers.

🌍

Culture & leadership

Global learning, language exchange and youth leadership programmes hosted through libraries.

Our collaborators

Partners making the KNLS vision possible

Explore the organisations supporting reading, digital access, inclusive services, cultural exchange and youth empowerment.

Books & reading

Book Aid International (BAI)

Opening doors through books

Book Aid International has helped KNLS bring shelves to life, especially in remote areas, through generous book donations that support children, students and communities to discover reading and knowledge.

Visit partner website →
Cultural exchange

U.S. Embassy to Kenya

Connecting cultures and ideas

The partnership with the U.S. Embassy has supported learning and cultural exchange through American Spaces in selected libraries, creating hubs where young Kenyans access digital tools, programmes and global perspectives.

Visit partner website →
Digital inclusion

Communications Authority of Kenya

Connecting communities

Through the support of the Communications Authority of Kenya, many KNLS libraries were connected to the digital world, particularly in underserved regions, strengthening inclusive access to ICT infrastructure.

Visit partner website →
Children & learning

The LEGO Foundation

Learning through play

The LEGO Foundation has helped introduce creative learning spaces where children learn through play, build imagination and explore problem-solving in joyful, curiosity-driven ways.

Visit partner website →
Infrastructure

Turkish Cooperation and Development Agency (TIKA)

Modernising spaces, strengthening communities

TIKA has supported the reimagining of library spaces through infrastructure upgrades, equipment and community programmes that make KNLS facilities more modern and welcoming.

Visit partner website →
Language & culture

Chinese Embassy and Hanban

Bridging cultures through language

The Chinese Embassy and Hanban have supported cultural exchange through Confucius Corners and Chinese learning resources, giving library users a window into language, culture and global understanding.

Chinese Embassy → Hanban →
Accessibility

Association for the Physically Disabled of Kenya (APDK)

Making libraries accessible to all

APDK has supported KNLS in creating spaces where people with disabilities feel seen and included, including support for adaptive technologies and more accessible physical spaces.

Visit partner website →
Inclusive reading

Kenya Society for the Blind (KSB)

Illuminating knowledge beyond sight

The Kenya Society for the Blind has supported access to braille materials, audio resources and assistive technologies, helping visually impaired readers access knowledge and participate fully.

Visit partner website →
Youth leadership

Young African Leaders Initiative – Regional Leadership Centre (YALI–RLC)

Inspiring young leaders

Through YALI–RLC, KNLS libraries have become spaces where young leaders can learn, collaborate and build skills that support community transformation and future leadership.

Visit partner website →
Mentorship

Knowledge Empowering Youth (KEY)

Equipping the next generation

Knowledge Empowering Youth has partnered with KNLS to support mentorship, information literacy training and digital resources for young people, encouraging informed, creative and confident problem-solvers.

Visit partner website →

Moving forward, together

Asante sana to every partner walking this journey with KNLS.

Each partnership tells a story of hope and impact—from a single donated book to a fully equipped digital hub. Together, we are not just building libraries; we are building futures.
